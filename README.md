## <img src="https://em-content.zobj.net/source/microsoft-teams/363/waving-hand_1f44b.png" width="28"> &nbsp; About me

```yaml
name:           Tahosin
role:           Full-stack / AI Developer
location:       Bangladesh
focus:          AI automation, developer tooling, free web apps
current_stack:  Next.js 16 · TypeScript · Google Gemini · Cloudflare · n8n · Solana
philosophy:     Ship products that are free, fast, and actually useful
writing:        579 views across 22 Dev.to articles and counting
side_quest:     Running a 6-platform auto-publisher that posts every 12 hours
```

I build AI-powered tools end-to-end — research, prompt engineering, UI, deployment, everything. Most of what I ship is free and open-source because the best way to learn AI is to have your hands on it, not to read blog posts about it.

Right now I am especially interested in **agentic workflows that run 24/7 without babysitting** (see my autopilot below), **browser regression testing with AI verification** (passmark), and **making AI genuinely useful for non-technical users** (MaxAI Writer, PromptCraft).
